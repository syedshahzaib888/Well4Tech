ABOUT US

About Us:

In 2022, Well4Tech emerged as a beacon of innovation and positive change in the realm of wellness-based technology. Founded by Yisroel Rabinowitz, a compassionate capitalist with an unwavering commitment to creating a better world, the company’s mission is as noble as it is visionary. Well4Tech is headquartered within one of Rabinowitz’s remarkable commercial real estate investments, known as The Bridge Healing Arts Center, nestled in the picturesque town of Farmington.

Fostering Global Innovation

Well4Tech’s impact transcends geographical boundaries. The company actively supports and unites startups on a global scale. By bringing together budding entrepreneurs, visionaries, and innovators, Well4Tech creates a dynamic ecosystem ripe for collaboration and growth.

Connecting Startups and Investors

One of Well4Tech’s key objectives is to facilitate the connection between startups and investors. By fostering networking and collaboration, Well4Tech bridges the gap between promising new ventures and those who believe in their potential. The result is a flourishing environment of support and opportunity.

Empowering through Bootcamps

In a bid to further empower startups, Well4Tech organizes a 3–5day bootcamp. These intensive programs are designed to provide the necessary tools, knowledge, and mentorship to help startups succeed. Through these bootcamps, Well4Tech ensures that emerging companies are well-prepared to take the next step in their journey.

The Grand Pitch
Day

The climax of Well4Tech’s support programs is the Grand Pitch Day. Startups, after receiving the guidance and mentorship they need, have the opportunity to showcase their vision to potential investors, consumers, and the wider public. It’s a day of transformation, where budding concepts take their first steps towards becoming reality.

The Bridge Healing Arts Center: A Hub of Innovation

The headquarters of Well4Tech, The Bridge Healing Arts Center, is more than just a physical location. It’s a symbol of convergence – where innovation, technology, and wellness intersect. Rabinowitz’s astute commercial real estate investment allows Well4Tech to draw inspiration from the serenity and healing ambience of The Bridge, reflecting the company’s core values.




Core Values
